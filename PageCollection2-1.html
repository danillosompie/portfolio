Collections>Experimental Photography


Theese pictures is an aplication of freeze motion photography technique. It is aim to catch the flying colorful plastic objects. The concept is to depict Indonesian proverbs "Di tengah keramaian, Aku merasa sepi". The meaning of the proverbs is some paradox situation. Plastics Balls symbolize Keramaian or Crowds. The dark background symbolize Kesepian or Loneliness.
