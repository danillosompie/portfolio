Redesign of Nusa Indah




Nusa Indah?

Rumah Makan Nusa Indah is a restaurant with a Nusantaran theme, beverages, main courses, and culinary treats from the eastern to the western regions of Indonesia. Products such as Batagor Kingsley, Mandarijn Orion, Sambal Bu Rudy, Es Krim Surabaya, traditional Javanese dishes, and many others can be found at this establishment. Unfortunately, as a business brand with a long track record and trusted by the public, Rumah Makan Nusa Indah still seems to be using traditional methods in the context of marketing and branding. Through observation during a visit, to review its visual identity directly, several issues were found. Redesign objective: New Logo, New Identity, and Efficient Branding.






Through creative and visual strategies that have been developed can be applied to appear appealing in the market. Nusa Indah's new big idea is to be a one stop shopping for Indonesian culinary. The new design expressing a tone and manner of simple, friendly, and clear.
