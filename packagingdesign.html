Nona Crispy Packaging Design

Nona Crispy is a UMKM/Micro brand, who sells keripik tempe in warung. The aim of this work is to create a rebranding for Nona Crispy and creating a holistic packaging ideas by continuation of packaging. So it can reach/approach the target markets and expanding their business by communicating through its package. It has three flavour Cheese, Orginal, and Spicy. Taking a keyword: Stories, each flavour has a unique story to tell. So the each flavour has their own visualization but yet it has a consistent and continuation.
