Collections>Sangkakala Bagi Kami


This poster addresses an environmental issue, specifically pollution. Its primary aim is to raise awareness and campaign against noise pollution, a problem frequently encountered by those living in metropolitan areas. Many people overlook how impactful noise pollution can be on our health. The loud sounds of modified exhaust systems, a growing trend among Jakarta’s youth, not only harm people's ears but also affect their mental well-being. In Jakarta, the noise from these exhausts has become a significant disturbance. The red color in the poster symbolizes anger and frustration toward this issue. The visualization includes exhaust pipes, ears, and a message written in Bahasa Indonesia that reads, “Karena Suaramu Sangkakala Bagi Kami.” This conveys the context and the message of the campaign effectively.
