Brand Identity for Nusa Indah


Rumah Makan Nusa Indah is a restaurant with a Nusantaran theme, beverages, main courses, and culinary treats from the eastern to the western regions of Indonesia. Products such as Batagor Kingsley, Mandarijn Orion, Sambal Bu Rudy, Zangrandi Ice Cream, traditional Javanese dishes, and many others can be found at this establishment. Unfortunately, as a business brand with a long track record and trusted by the public, Rumah Makan Nusa Indah still seems to be using traditional methods in the context of marketing and branding. Through observation during a visit, to review its visual identity directly, several issues were found. Rebranding objective: New Logo, New Identity, and Efficient Branding.





The Identity

Through a research process that included interviews, Nusa Indah's identity was summarized into the Big Idea: "One Stop Shopping for Indonesian Culinary" as a strategy to position Nusa Indah as a solution for those seeking Indonesian culinary treats in Jakarta. The new design strategy expressing a tone and manner of simple, friendly, and clear.






Take a closer looks on how's the logo depict one stop shopping


Several key words were selected as the foundation for designing Nusa Indah's new logo identity:

Center: Not only does it serve as center point for culinary treats from the eastern to the western parts of Indonesia, but it also a center point between these products and their customers. (Compas)

Easy: Providing ease for those searching for culinary treats in Jakarta and traditional Indonesian dishes. Through its products, Nusa Indah addresses the community's needs for meaningful communication, as culinary treats are often used as a medium to express affection and serve as thoughtful gifts for various occasions.

Nusantara: Reflecting the rich cultural themes of Indonesia. (Batik Kawung Pattern)


Stoples or jars from all directions resemble a compass

What represents a culinary treats store? One of my inspiring visits revealed that "Stoples" or jars, as display vessels in the store, became a defining element. These jars symbolize and represent the essence of a shop for culinary treats.
